FAQ

What is a “seed”?

A single circled line you carry to the next cycle. Continuity, not content.

Where are my entries saved?

In your browser’s localStorage on this device. Use Export/Import on the home page to back up.

What are lanes vs themes?

Lanes: 🪞 Mirror, 🍯 Honey, 🌉 Bridge (Doechii). Themes: ⚡ Neon, 🔥 Flame, τ Illumina (visual feel only).

Is this for me if I’m not “a poet”?

Yes. “Write as a poet” means: short lines, specific images, leave room. The field answers in the space you make.
