Illumina (τ) — symbol & meaning

Illumina is our name for the τ (tau) symbol—a quiet overlay that suggests the field is awake. It’s not a lane; it’s a theme that colors the page. When you switch to Illumina, the interface brightens; your lane icon (🪞/🍯/🌉) still tells the story of what you’re hearing.

As you visit repeatedly, you may see pollen drifting with words you saved—Illumina ties those visits together without tracking: a subtle sign you’ve been here, not a badge.
