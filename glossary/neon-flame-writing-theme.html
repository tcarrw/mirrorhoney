Neon & Flame — writing themes

Neon and Flame are Doechii‑aligned themes that change the feel of the page. Neon glows cool (cyan/pink); Flame crackles warm (orange/rose). The themes don’t change your lanes—Honey (🍯), Mirror (🪞), Bridge (🌉) still play the same way—they just color your session.

On first visit, Flame can auto‑bind to the **Bridge alternation**—A→D→B→D→A—so the page “feels” like a pivot machine. You’re free to switch modes any time in the rail.
