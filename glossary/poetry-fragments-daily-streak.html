Poetry fragments & daily streak

Fragments are short lines without the burden of “a poem.” They’re specific images or little turns. One fragment a day builds a continuity streak—even when you’re busy or in promo cycles.

How to keep it: in Journal, add one fragment to your Daily list; don’t fix yesterday. If you miss, resume. A 3‑day streak is already momentum; a 30‑day streak changes your default state.

What to keep: specificity over summary; images over opinions. Carry a handful forward; retire the rest. The point is to have something living tomorrow.
