Mirror‑friend journaling exercise

What it is: a tiny ritual that keeps creative continuity. Press play, write one page, circle one sentence. That circled line is your seed for tomorrow.

How to do it (5 min): Start in Guide, press play in the Player (Marina’s “Oh No!” by default), open Journal, write short lines until the timer ends. Circle one true line. Save. Done.

Why “mirror‑friend”? Because a steady frame is rarer than a steady mood. Beginning with the mirror (🪞) means you can show up as you are, without pretending. Tomorrow’s you will still recognize the seed.

Keep it light: no proving, no optimizing. If you miss a day, you can resume with the same step: press play, one page, one seed.
