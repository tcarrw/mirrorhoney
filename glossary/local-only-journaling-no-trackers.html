Local‑only journaling (no trackers)

The practice is private by default. Entries, fragments, and queue live in your browser’s localStorage. No accounts, no analytics, no background calls. Export when you want a backup; import if you switch devices.

Where to find it: the export/import controls are in the right rail. The page behaves like a small app without a server. (Quiet Moon uses the same clarity approach—one page, transparent head tags, clear meta—carry that here.)
