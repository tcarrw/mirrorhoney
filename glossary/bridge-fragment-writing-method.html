Bridge fragment writing method

A bridge fragment is a tiny lever: 3–7 words that flip the frame. It lives in the Bridge (🌉) lane—our Doechii energy. It’s not a full lyric; it’s a pivot you can carry into the next pass.

How: while a track plays, write short, unrhymed fragments. Stop after one that jolts the page (e.g., “sideways cherry neon”). Save it to Daily fragments. Tomorrow, start from that fragment and either sweeten (🍯) or sharpen (🪞).

Why it works: fragments escape perfectionism. They move you across energy states—Honey ↔ Mirror—without heavy lifting. The loop stays alive.
