Day 1 – Unclench

Notice what’s still buzzing. Turn off push notifications. Write one sentence: what am I still holding that isn’t mine? Exhale it out loud once.

Day 2 – Clear Channels

Tidy one small space — desk, tabs, or photos. Don’t add new media; let silence be air. Take a slow walk beneath open sky.

Day 3 – Listen Back

Revisit something you made weeks ago. No edits; simply notice where energy still moves. Mark those lines with a small dot • —they’re seeds.

Day 4 – Still Waters

Speak only when warmth is required. Hold a cup of something warm; watch light shift across a wall. Let your words dissolve into observation.

Day 5 – Dream Drift

Sleep without alarms. Note the first image or phrase that appears when you wake. That’s your internal frequency check.

Day 6 – Re-tone

Hum a single sustained note. Feel where it vibrates. No lyrics — just resonance and breath.

Day 7 – Re-illumination Threshold

Write again: what do I now hold that is mine? Open the light — music, color, movement. You’ve completed the fold; the mirror reopens.
