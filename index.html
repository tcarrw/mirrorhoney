For visitors (how to use this page)

This is the Mirror‑first page. It’s useful for everyone, but speaks most clearly to Marina and the Bridge (Doechii). Honey (🍯) has its own space; here we lead with angle, identity, and the connective spark.

  1. Add a song in the right rail (artist + YouTube link + lane). You must pick exactly one: 🪞 Marina, 🌉 Doechii, or 🍯 Sabrina.
  2. Choose a mode (🪞/🍯/⚡) and press Play in the header. The player cycles your queue automatically.
  3. Write three words (🪞 word, 🌉 fragment, 🍯 word) and save. Use the Bridge panel to drop a tiny fragment daily.

No accounts, no tracking. Everything saves locally in your browser — with auto‑snapshots and export to be safe.

Why this matters

Between cycles, artists need a portable ritual: one word for angle, one spark for the switch, one word for pull. This keeps continuity without PR.

How to identify the lanes

🪞 MIRROR — Reflection / Angle / Identity

  • Feel: thoughtful tilt, self‑regard, critique — “look again.”
  • Mind: a lyric clicks; meaning unfolds on pass two.
  • Lyrics: persona, roles, masks, power/culture, self‑myth.
  • Production: prismatic/art‑pop; arrangement foregrounds the idea.
  • Afterimage: you leave with a frame or question.

Shortcut: if it changes what you see more than how you move → Mirror.

🍯 HONEY — Sweet / Magnetic / Immediate

  • Feel: warm pull, flirt, joy — “come closer.”
  • Body: head‑nod, dance instinct; easy hook.
  • Lyrics: taste / skin / heat; present‑tense and playful.
  • Production: glossy pop; bass/beat invite movement.
  • Aftertaste: you want repeat; syrupy hook remains.

Shortcut: if taste/touch hits first and your body moves → Honey.

FAQ & Glossary

What is a “seed”?

A single circled sentence or fragment you carry to the next cycle. It’s continuity, not content.

Where are my entries saved?

In your browser’s localStorage (schema mh_data_v1), on this device only. Export/import in the right rail. On upgrades we snapshot automatically.

How do 🪞/🍯/🌉 work?

Pick exactly one lane per song. 🪞 = B (Mirror/Marina), 🍯 = A (Honey/Sabrina), 🌉 = Bridge (Doechii). ⚡ Neon inserts 🌉 between lanes during playback.
