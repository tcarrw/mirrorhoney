What this is (and why)

Problem: Big artists have attention. What they lack is continuity when promo ends, a peer mirror outside the machine, and a place to play without PR stakes.

Solution: A gentle rhythm you can keep: listen, notice (Honey or Mirror?), write one page, circle one seed. That seed carries forward to the next cycle.

Promise: No asks, no pressure, no tracking. Private logs stay in your browser.

This is not
  • Outreach, clout, or press.
  • A fan-control lever or rumor mill.
  • A definition of anyone. It’s a mirror and a page of honey.
Privacy

Entries save locally in your browser (localStorage). You can export at any time.

How to identify the two halves

🍯 HONEY — Sweet / Magnetic / Immediate

  • Feel: warm pull, flirt, joy — “come closer.”
  • Body: head-nod, dance instinct; easy hook.
  • Lyrics: taste / skin / heat; present-tense and playful.
  • Production: glossy pop; bass/beat invite movement.
  • Aftertaste: you want repeat; syrupy hook remains.

Shortcut: if taste/touch hits first and your body moves → Honey.

🪞 MIRROR — Reflection / Angle / Identity

  • Feel: thoughtful tilt, self-regard, critique — “look again.”
  • Mind: a lyric clicks; meaning unfolds on pass two.
  • Lyrics: persona, roles, masks, power/culture, self-myth.
  • Production: prismatic/art-pop; arrangement foregrounds the idea.
  • Afterimage: you leave with a frame or question.

Shortcut: if it changes what you see more than how you move → Mirror.

How a Honey × Mirror hybrid feels

Honey layer

  • Immediate groove, singable chorus.
  • Taste/heat/skin imagery.
  • Pop sheen nudges you to move.

Mirror layer

  • Bridge flips perspective (mask ↔ mirror).
  • Verse introduces identity/power tension.
  • A prismatic break spotlights the idea.

FAQ & Glossary

What is a “seed”?

A single circled sentence (Honey) or fragment (Chaos) you carry to the next cycle. It’s continuity, not content.

Where are my entries saved?

In your browser’s localStorage, on this device only. Use Export in the queue dock to back up.

How do A / B / D tags work?

A = Honey-primary lane, B = Mirror-primary lane, D = Doechii lane. Filter the queue to focus; each player advances in its own lane.
