You refuse to be contained. Your spirit lives in open air.

Act III — Empowerment

You rewrite the law in your own voice. “Ancient Dreams in a Modern Land” ignites purpose.

Shadow becomes play. Fear bows to rhythm. “Final Boss” smiles back — because it’s you, finally whole.

Act IV — Ascension

Freedom quiets. “Purge the Poison” clears the air. “Power & Control” finds balance.

You’re not escaping anymore — you’re breathing. The open air becomes home. The hum remains.
